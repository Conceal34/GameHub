Tic Tac Toe

Casual - Free

Tic-tac-toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking spaces with "X" or "O", aiming to be the first to get three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). It's a simple game often used to introduce strategic thinking and game theory.

Pac Man

Casual - Free

Pac-Man is a classic arcade game where the player controls Pac-Man, a yellow, pizza-shaped character, through a maze, eating dots and power pellets while avoiding four ghosts: Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde. When Pac-Man eats a power pellet, the ghosts turn blue, and he can eat them for extra points. The goal is to clear each maze of dots and advance to the next level.

Simon Game

Casual - Free

Simon is an electronic game of memory skill where players must repeat an ever-increasing sequence of lights and sounds by pressing the corresponding colored buttons.

Drum Kit
